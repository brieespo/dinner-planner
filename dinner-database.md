# Dinner Database
*Last updated: June 20, 2026*

**Planning system:** Pick 5 from this list on Sunday → place Whole Foods order → no deciding during the week.

**When planning, maximize shared ingredients:** choose meals that overlap on produce, proteins, and pantry items to reduce waste and simplify the order. Examples:
- Parmesan appears in: orecchiette, cheesy orzo, gnocchi w/ broccoli, beans & greens vodka, gnocchi w/ peas — pick 2–3 in the same week
- Shredded chicken: halal cart chicken → leftover into peanut chicken chop next night
- Cherry tomatoes: baked feta pasta + garlic butter angel hair (salmon night)
- Goat cheese: gnocchi w/ broccoli + frittata
- Fennel/shallots: Hot Sheet sausage + Alison Roman chicken dishes
- Baby spinach: stewed chicken dumplings + beans & greens vodka + frittata + chicken curry + spinach artichoke chicken bake
- Tahini: Alison Roman cauliflower + cauliflower lentil salad (Flavors of the Sun) + beef lentil bowl
- Dates: Alison Roman cauliflower + Nothing Fancy chicken + cauliflower lentil salad — buy a big bag
- Soy sauce + chili garlic: sticky chicken + miso-honey chicken + gingery ground beef
- Sour cream: spinach artichoke chicken bake + stewed chicken dumplings + enchilada skillet
- Fresh mint + parsley: salmon kebabs + garlicky chicken couscous + cauliflower lentil salad

**Labels:** 🟢 Quick weeknight (under 45 min) | 🟡 Moderate (up to 90 min) | 🔴 Weekend/slow day only | ⭐ Daughter-approved | 🥗 Fiber | 🥑 Healthy fats

## Flavors of the Sun — *Christine Benlafquih*

### Salmon Kebabs with Nigella ⭐⭐ 🟡 🥑
*Page 132 — grill or broil; "one of my favorite salmon recipes ever" (Bri's note)*
**Marinade:** center-cut skinless salmon fillet (2 lbs, cut into 2-inch chunks), fresh parsley (2 tbsp), fresh mint (2 tbsp), fresh chives (2 tbsp), Aleppo pepper (2 tsp), ground cumin (1 tsp), nigella seeds (½ tsp), olive oil (½ cup), salt & black pepper
**To grill:** thread onto skewers alternating with red onion wedges; grill or broil 2–3 min per side
**Serve with:** lemon wedges, pilaf, pita with pomegranate vinaigrette, or arugula

### Roasted Cauliflower Salad with Lentils and Dates 🟡 🥗 🥑
*Page 306 — hearty salad, can be a main*
**Ingredients:** cauliflower (1 head, cut into florets), cumin, cinnamon, ginger, cayenne or Urfa pepper, olive oil, lemon juice (3 tbsp), tahini (2 tbsp), honey (1 tsp), cooked green lentils (1 cup), scallions, spinach/baby arugula/romaine (2 cups), dates (10, pitted and chopped), roasted unsalted almonds (½ cup), fresh mint

### Beef and Lentil Bowl with Tahini Dressing 🟡 🥗
*Page 262 — shawarma-spiced beef over lentil rice, crispy onions are key*
**Ingredients:** beef top round (1 lb, cut into thin strips), shawarma spices, dry red wine (1 tbsp), olive oil, large onion (1, thinly sliced into rings — fry until crispy), French lentils (2 cups), jasmine rice (1 cup), ground allspice, salt & black pepper
**Tahini dressing:** tahini (½ cup), lemon juice (¼ cup), fresh parsley (1 tbsp), minced garlic (1 tsp)
**Serve with:** Persian cucumbers, pickled vegetables, grape tomatoes
