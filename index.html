How To Use Dinner Planner

Building Your Weekly Plan

The home screen gives you two ways to start your week. Randomize auto-fills all 7 days at once based on your day rules and seasons. Start Blank gives you an empty grid to fill in yourself — each empty card has a Suggest button to fill just that slot, and a + Pick button to choose manually. You can also add sides, desserts, breakfast, and lunch to any day using the buttons below each main course card.

Saved Menus

Click Saved Menus in the header to save and revisit menus you've loved.

  • Weekly Menus: After building a plan you like, click Save This Week at the top of the plan grid. Give it a name (or keep the date default) and it's saved as a card. Click any saved card to load that week back into your plan, or to delete it.
  • Event Menus: Build a custom menu for a party or gathering — search and pick recipes from your collection, or add one-off dishes on the fly. Saved as a card you can reopen anytime.

All Recipes

Click All Recipes in the header to browse your full collection. Use the tab strip to filter by category (Mains, Sides, Desserts, etc.), or use the search bar and dropdowns to filter by cook time, tag, season, or status. Click any recipe card to view its full ingredients and instructions.

Community

Click Community in the header to browse recipes shared by other users. Click any card to preview it, then hit Import to add it to your own collection. Set your display name under ⋯ More → Set Display Name so your recipes show up with your name attached.

Adding Recipes

  • From a URL: Click + Add Recipe, paste a link to any recipe blog in the import field, and hit Import. The form will auto-fill with the recipe's name, ingredients, and instructions.
  • Manually: Fill in the form fields yourself. Every recipe needs a name and at least one ingredient. The Type field (Main, Side, Dessert, etc.) determines which tab it appears under in All Recipes.

Favorites & Made It

Click the star on any recipe card to mark it as a favorite — the card gets a gold highlight. Click + Made it to log that you've cooked a recipe. Use the status filter in All Recipes to view only Favorites, Made It recipes, or ones you haven't tried yet.

Grocery List

After building your plan, click Grocery List to see all ingredients for the week organized by category (produce, protein, dairy, etc.). Check off items as you shop.

Day Rules

Under ⋯ More → Day Rules, you can set preferences for each day of the week — for example, "Meatless Monday" or "Quick meals on weeknights." These guide the Suggest and Randomize features when auto-filling your plan.

Tags

Tags let you label recipes with custom categories like "kid-friendly," "vegetarian," or "fiber." Manage your tag list under ⋯ More → Tags. You can filter by tag in All Recipes.

Export & Backup

Use ⋯ More → Export Recipes to download a backup of your entire recipe database as a JSON file. This is useful for safekeeping or for sharing your collection.

Your Account

Sign in with your email to sync your recipes and plan across devices. Your data is saved to the cloud automatically whenever you make changes.

Set Display Name
