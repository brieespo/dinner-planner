*Last updated: June 21, 2026 — living document, refine as needed*

## Morning Routine

### Skincare (every day, ~3 min at vanity)
1. Alpha arbutin serum OR Caudalie Vinoperfect (antioxidants/brighteners work best before SPF)
2. Moisturizer — Cicaplast Balm B5+ or Illiyoon Ceramide (skip if skin feels fine)
3. SPF — Elta MD Clear or Tatcha silk sunscreen (rotate or use whichever you grab)

**On Tuesday and Thursday** (must be ready by 8:30am): do this in under 2 minutes. Skip moisturizer unless skin is very dry. SPF only is fine.

### Chores
- **Dishwasher** — unload and/or reload (already automatic; protect this habit)
- **Laundry** — start one load before you leave or before class; switch to dryer at midday; fold and put away immediately when done
  - *ADHD note: fold the moment the dryer stops — do not let it sit or it becomes a mountain*

## Nightly Skincare Routine

**Every night, always:**
- Remove makeup with micellar water (skip only if you did a full cleanse)
- End with Cicaplast Balm B5+ or Illiyoon Ceramide

**Cleanse nights (Herbivore Pink Cloud + Foreo):** Wednesday, Friday, Sunday
- Cleanse replaces micellar water — don't do both

| Night | Cleanse? | Treatment | Notes |
|-------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Monday** | Micellar water | Sunday Riley Luna 🌙 | Retinol night |
| **Tuesday** | Micellar water | Kiehl's Midnight Recovery oil | Nourishing buffer night |
| **Wednesday** | Herbivore + Foreo | **Chanel Le Lift sample** 🌙 | Retinol on clean skin — good for trialing |
| **Thursday** | Micellar water | Kiehl's Midnight Recovery oil | Nourishing buffer night |
| **Friday** | Herbivore + Foreo | Sunday Riley Luna 🌙 | Retinol night; longer self-care window |
| **Saturday** | Micellar water | Nothing, or Kiehl's oil | Low maintenance night |
| **Sunday** | Herbivore + Foreo | Sunday Riley Luna 🌙 | Retinol night |

🌙 = retinol (Luna or Chanel Le Lift sample) — never use two retinols in the same week slot

**Retinol schedule:** Mon / Wed / Fri / Sun = 3–4x weekly. If skin feels irritated, drop to Mon / Fri / Sun and add Kiehl's oil on the off nights.

**Once Chanel Le Lift sample is assessed:** if you like it more than Luna, swap it into the Friday slot (wash night, more time, less rushed). If it's too strong, shelve it and stick to Luna.

## Laundry System

**Goal:** one load per day, completed start-to-finish (not left in dryer overnight)

| Step | When |
|------|------|
| Start load | Morning, before leaving or before first class |
| Switch to dryer | Midday break or between classes |
| Fold + put away | Same day, immediately when dry |

*Tip: If you can't fold right away, set a timer when the dryer stops. Folding a warm load is faster than folding a cold pile.*

**What counts as one load:** anything that fills the machine — don't save laundry for a "big laundry day." Small loads daily = never behind.
